Research

Research Interests

  • Cybersecurity
  • Machine Learning
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • AI Security

Purpose of the Interests

- Cybersecurity is crucial for protecting sensitive data and systems from cyber threats and attacks. Research in this area aims to develop advanced techniques to safeguard digital infrastructure, ensuring the privacy and integrity of information in an increasingly connected world.
- Machine learning innovation across various domains by enabling systems to learn from data and improve over time. Research focuses on developing algorithms and models that can efficiently process and analyze large datasets, leading to intelligent decision-making and automation.
- NLP is essential for enabling computers to understand and interact with human language. Research in NLP aims to improve language models, translation systems, and sentiment analysis tools, enhancing communication and information retrieval in diverse applications.
- Computer vision is key for interpreting and understanding visual data. Research in this field seeks to develop algorithms for image and video analysis, enabling advancements in areas such as autonomous vehicles, medical imaging, and surveillance.
- AI security critical for ensuring that artificial intelligence systems are robust, reliable, and secure. Research focuses on identifying vulnerabilities, developing defenses against adversarial attacks, and creating frameworks for safe AI deployment in various industries.
