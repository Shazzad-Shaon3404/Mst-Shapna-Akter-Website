Biography

Mst Shapna Akter is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Oakland University. She completed her PhD at the University of West Florida in the Department of Intelligent Systems and Robotics, where she received an Outstanding PhD Student Award. She holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering, graduating with Cum Laude distinction. Her research focuses on machine learning (ML), quantum machine learning (QML), and deep learning (DL) applications, particularly in the cybersecurity and healthcare domains. Her research has been recognized and published in prestigious international conferences and journals, such as IEEE SERVICES, IEEE CNS, IEEE BigData, IEEE COMPSAC, and Elsevier. She has over 25 publications with 125+ citations on Google Scholar. She has also served as a program committee member and session chair at IEEE BigData, IEEE SERVICES, and IEEE COMPSAC.

Dr. Shapna's research interests include:

  • Smart Healthcare
  • AI Security
  • Learning based System Design, Analysis and Prediction
